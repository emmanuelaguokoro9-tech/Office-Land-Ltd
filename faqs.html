Frequently Asked Questions

You can place an order through our website by browsing our product catalog, selecting the items you need, and proceeding to checkout. You'll need to provide your shipping address and payment information to complete the purchase. For bulk orders, you can contact our corporate sales team directly.

You can modify or cancel your order within 1 hour of placing it by contacting our customer support team. Once the order has been processed for shipping, modifications may not be possible. Please contact us immediately if you need to make changes.

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email and SMS. You can track your order on our website using the Track Your Order page or directly through the courier's website.

We offer standard and express shipping options across Nigeria. Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days, while express delivery takes 1-2 business days. Free shipping is available on orders above ₦100,000.

Currently, we primarily serve customers within Nigeria. For international inquiries, please contact our support team to discuss your specific needs and shipping options.

Shipping costs depend on your location and the size/weight of your order. Standard shipping rates start at ₦2,500 for small items. Orders over ₦100,000 qualify for free shipping within Lagos, Abuja, and Port Harcourt.

We offer a 30-day return policy on all items. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can return it within 30 days of delivery for a full refund. Items must be in their original condition with all packaging and accessories.

To initiate a return, please contact our customer support team with your order number and reason for return. We'll provide you with a return authorization and shipping instructions. Visit our Returns & Refunds page for more details.

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive and inspect your return. The refund will be issued to your original payment method. Bank transfers may take an additional 2-3 business days to reflect in your account.

Yes, all our products come with a comprehensive warranty. Furniture items typically have a 5-year warranty, while electronics and technology products come with a 1-2 year manufacturer's warranty. Specific warranty details are provided on each product page.

Yes, we offer custom furniture solutions for corporate clients. Our design team can work with you to create bespoke pieces that match your brand identity and workspace requirements. Contact our corporate sales team to discuss your needs.

Most of our furniture products are delivered with assembly instructions. We also offer professional installation services for an additional fee. Our team can assemble and set up your furniture for a seamless experience.

We accept various payment methods including:

  • Credit and Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, Verve)
  • Bank Transfers
  • USSD Payments
  • Corporate Purchase Orders (for businesses)

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security protocols to protect your payment information. Our payment gateway is PCI DSS compliant, ensuring your data is safe and secure.

Yes, we offer installment payment options for qualifying customers through our partner financial institutions. Contact our support team for more information about installment plans and eligibility criteria.

You can create an account by clicking on the "Account" icon in the top right corner of our website and selecting "Create Account." You'll need to provide your email address, name, and create a password. Existing customers can also create accounts to track orders and manage preferences.

Click on the "Account" icon and select "Forgot Password." Enter your registered email address, and we'll send you a password reset link. Follow the instructions in the email to create a new password.

Yes, we offer competitive pricing for bulk orders. Corporate clients can benefit from volume discounts, custom pricing, and dedicated account management. Contact our corporate sales team for a customized quote.

Yes, we provide samples for corporate clients before placing bulk orders. Sample availability depends on the product type. Contact our corporate sales team to arrange sample delivery.
